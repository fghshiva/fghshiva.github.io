About me

With a background in Electrical and Biomedical Engineering, I completed my PhD in Computational Neuroscience at Dartmouth College where I studied the computational principles and neural mechanisms of Reinforcement Learning in the brain. After finishing my PhD, I joined Flatiron Institute to study the connection between biologically inspired neural networks and Representation Learning in the brain. Currently, I am a Senior machine learning engineer at Harbinger Health, Inc. a bioplatform company focused on detection of early cancer. You can find more about me here. Please contact me via:
