About me

I am a Research Fellow at Flatiron institute. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Psychological and Brain Sciences at Dartmouth College. I also hold a master's degree in Biomedical Engineering and a bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering. My research focuses on detailed computational models that provide feasible mechanisms for both behavioral and neuronal data. You can find more about me here. Please contact me via:
